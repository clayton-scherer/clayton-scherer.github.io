My Works in Progress

Here are some of the projects I have been working on! All projects were developed using an Agile work methodology, test driven development and of course, clean code!

High st. Hospital

This was a console based application that mimicked a hospital database. The user started out viewing a custom menu that they could navigate to see: records for the staff and their abilities, the patients and their statuses, as well as being able to exit at their leisure.

Virtual Pet

This was a console based application that allowed the player to interact with virtual pets through our shelter system. Players could feed, play and bathe their pets while making sure to keep the shelter and their pets cages clean!

Welp Review Site

This was a fullstack MVC application that mocked a review site, similar to Yelp, but with memes. Users had the ability to create tags, categories and authors as well as the ability to post reviews.
